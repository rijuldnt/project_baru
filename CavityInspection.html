CAVITY INSPECTION

We provide dental care cavity inspection for finding cysts, tumors, or
abscesses in the oral cavity, checking the location of prospective permanent
teeth growing in the jaw in children who still have milk teeth, or. plan
therapy to correct misaligned teeth.

WHAT IS THE PURPOSE OF CAVITY

INSPECTION ?

This treatment aims to provide care and check the health of the mouth and
teeth. For example, to check the cause of toothache symptoms, the condition
of the gums, wisdom teeth, cavities, cleaning tartar, or if the patient
wants to install braces or false teeth.
